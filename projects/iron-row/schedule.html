CLASS TYPES

Shadow Boxing

Perfect your form and footwork without a partner. Build muscle memory and conditioning.

Heavy Bag

Develop power and endurance on the bag. Focus on combinations, timing, and rotational force.

Sparring

Controlled live rounds with coaching oversight. Gear required. All experience levels welcome.

Pad Work

One-on-one mitt drills to sharpen accuracy, speed, and defensive reactions with a coach.

HIIT / Strength

High-intensity conditioning and functional strength training built for combat athletes.

Open Gym

Unstructured training time. Bags, rings, and equipment available. Coaches on site.
