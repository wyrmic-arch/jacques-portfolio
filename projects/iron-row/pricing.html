FAQ

Is there a contract?

No. All plans are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No hidden fees.

Can I try before I join?

Absolutely. We offer a free 7-day trial with full access to all group classes and open gym hours.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. You can change plans at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at next billing cycle.

Is equipment provided?

Yes. Bags, gloves, wraps, and pads are all available at the gym. We recommend bringing your own gear for hygiene.
