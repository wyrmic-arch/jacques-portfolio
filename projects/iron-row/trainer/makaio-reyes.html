MAKAIO REYES

"The Hammer" — Heavyweight Specialist

BIO

Makaio "The Hammer" Reyes is a heavyweight specialist with a 14-0 amateur record and a Golden Gloves championship. After a decade in the ring, he transitioned to coaching, bringing the same intensity that made him a feared competitor. His fighters describe his sessions as equal parts technical mastery and psychological warfare. "Boxing is chess with concussions," he says. Makaio works with everyone from pro contenders to first-timers, tailoring his approach to each fighter's strengths.
