The Neighborhood

Ember & Ash sits on a quiet stretch of Ash Street in Portland’s Richmond neighborhood, a pocket of the city where craft coffee shops, independent bookstores, and century-old elms line the blocks. We are a short walk from the Hawthorne Bridge and within easy reach of Division Street’s restaurant row. Street parking is available, and we encourage bikes—there is a rack just outside the door. Come as you are. Leave when the embers die down.
