Dining Policies

Everything you need to know before you visit

Cancellation

We kindly ask for 24 hours’ notice if you need to cancel or modify your reservation. Late cancellations and no-shows may be subject to a $50 per person fee.

Dress Code

Smart casual is welcome. We ask that guests refrain from wearing strongly scented products, as they can interfere with the experience of the food and wine.

Dietary Restrictions

We accommodate most allergies and dietary restrictions with advance notice. Please include details in your special requests when booking, and confirm with your server upon arrival.

Private Dining

Our mezzanine table seats up to ten guests for a prix fixe experience. For inquiries, please email private@emberandash.com or call the restaurant.
