Our Story

From a small family firm to one of the region's most trusted developers.

Since 1998

Built on a Foundation of Trust

Vertex was founded in Toronto by Edward Chen with a single crew and a steadfast belief: that great buildings begin with integrity. Twenty-seven years later, we have grown into a vertically integrated development and construction firm with over 1,200 professionals and a portfolio spanning residential, commercial, and mixed-use projects.

Our projects have shaped neighbourhoods, earned LEED certifications, and won international design awards. But what sets Vertex apart is not just what we build — it's how we build. Every project is a partnership built on transparency, collaboration, and an uncompromising commitment to quality.

We don't just construct buildings. We create lasting landmarks.

— Edward Chen, Founder & CEO

Our Principles

What We Stand For

The values that guide every decision, every project, every relationship.

Innovation

From sustainable materials to smart building systems, we continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in construction.
