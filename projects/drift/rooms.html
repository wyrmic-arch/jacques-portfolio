A Room for Every Dream

From intimate garden hideaways to sprawling cliffside villas, every stay at Drift is uniquely memorable.

Suite

Ocean Suite

$950 / night

Wake to the sound of waves and the golden glow of sunrise over the Pacific. The Ocean Suite is our flagship accommodation, offering an expansive living space wrapped in floor-to-ceiling glass. A private balcony extends your living area outdoors, where the salt breeze and panoramic views become part of your daily ritual.

The king-size bed is dressed in Italian linens, while the marble bathroom features a deep soaking tub positioned to capture the ocean vista through a picture window. A separate rain shower, dual vanities, and heated floors complete the experience.

Villa

Cliff Villa

$1,800 / night

Suspended on the edge of the cliff, the Cliff Villa is the ultimate expression of coastal luxury. This two-bedroom residence features a private infinity pool that appears to merge with the ocean horizon, an outdoor shower, and a sun terrace with handwoven loungers.

Inside, a great room with vaulted ceilings opens onto the terrace through bi-fold glass doors. The master suite includes a standalone bathtub facing the sea, while the second bedroom offers flexibility for families or traveling companions. A personal butler is assigned to every villa.

Room

Garden Room

$550 / night

For those who find peace among the leaves, the Garden Rooms offer a ground-level retreat nestled within Drift's lush tropical gardens. Each room opens onto a private patio shaded by mature palms and flowering bougainvillea, with an outdoor daybed perfect for afternoon reading.

Interiors are warm and grounding, with natural textures of rattan, linen, and reclaimed wood. The en-suite bathroom features an open-air shower surrounded by ferns, blurring the line between indoors and nature. Garden Rooms are our most intimate and affordable accommodation.
