Experiences

Curated adventures and quiet moments — discover the many faces of Drift.

Curated by Drift

The Art of Doing & Being

Whether you seek adrenaline or stillness, our experiences are designed to connect you with the coast and yourself.

Adventure

Surfing the Pacific

Ride world-class breaks just steps from the hotel. Our resident surf guides offer private lessons for beginners and guided sessions for experienced surfers seeking the best swells. All equipment provided.

Cuisine

Coastal Wine Tastings

Sample the region's finest vintages during sunset tastings on our ocean terrace. Sommelier-led sessions feature local vineyards and rare coastal labels, paired with artisanal cheeses and freshly shucked oysters.

Wellness

Sunrise Yoga

Begin each day with vinyasa flow on the cliffside platform, as the sun rises over the Pacific. Suitable for all levels, our sessions are led by experienced instructors who tailor each practice to the energy of the morning.

Cuisine

Farm-to-Table Dining

Our restaurant sources from local farms and daily-caught seafood. Menus change with the seasons, and our chef's tasting table offers an intimate look at the craft behind every dish.

Signature

The Drift Itinerary

Let us craft a bespoke schedule for your stay, blending relaxation and discovery in perfect measure.
