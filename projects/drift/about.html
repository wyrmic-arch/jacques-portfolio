Our Story

The vision behind Drift and the people who bring it to life.

A Vision Born from the Sea

Drift was born from a simple belief: that the most profound luxury is found not in excess, but in alignment with nature. Founded in 2018 by architect Elena Vasquez and hospitality veteran Marcus Chen, Drift occupies a stretch of coastline that has been carefully preserved for generations.

The land — a rugged headland where the Santa Lucia Mountains meet the Pacific — was once a private family estate. Elena and Marcus spent two years designing a resort that would touch the earth lightly, working with local craftspeople, sustainable builders, and indigenous plant specialists to create something that felt less like a hotel and more like a permanent state of peace.

Every beam, every textile, every plate in our restaurant has been chosen with intention. We source from within 50 miles wherever possible. We built with reclaimed materials. We employ local artisans and pay living wages. Luxury, for us, is not about what you have — it's about how you feel.

— Elena Vasquez & Marcus Chen
