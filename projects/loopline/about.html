About Loopline

The story behind the platform that's changing how teams work.

Our Story

Built by a team that couldn't stand context switching.

Loopline was founded in 2023 by a group of engineers and designers who had grown tired of juggling ten different tools just to get work done. We were using Jira for tickets, Notion for docs, Slack for communication, Figma for design, and a dozen other apps — each with its own login, its own notifications, and its own way of doing things. The friction was real, and it was slowing us down. We built Loopline to be the single workspace that connects everything: tasks, docs, tools, and people — all in one place.

What started as an internal tool quickly caught the attention of other teams facing the same frustrations. We launched our private beta in early 2024 with 50 teams, and by the end of the year over 2,000 organizations were using Loopline to streamline their workflows. Today, we're backed by some of the best investors in the space, but our mission remains the same: build a platform that respects your time, reduces complexity, and helps your team focus on the work that actually matters.

Our Values

What drives every decision we make.

These principles guide how we build, support, and grow.

🎯

Focus Over Features

We'd rather do one thing exceptionally well than ten things poorly. Every feature ships because it solves a real problem, not because it's trendy.

🔒

Radical Transparency

We're open about our roadmap, our pricing, and our mistakes. Trust is earned by being honest — with our users and within our team.

⚡

Speed as a Feature

Fast doesn't mean rushed. We optimize for performance in everything we build — from our interface to our API to our support response times.

🤝

Customer Obsession

Every support ticket is read by an engineer. Every feature request is reviewed. Our users shape our roadmap, not internal hunches.

🌱

Sustainable Growth

We grow deliberately, not at all costs. That means a healthy work-life balance for our team and a sustainable business model for our users.

🌍

Inclusive by Design

Great tools should work for everyone. We design for accessibility, support multiple languages, and offer pricing that works for teams of all sizes.
